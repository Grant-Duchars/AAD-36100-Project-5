Our Solar System

Mercury

The first planet from the sun and the smallest planet in our solar system.

Venus

The second planet from the sun and a close relative to Earth.

Earth

The third planet from the sun and the only known planet to house life.

Mars

The forth planet from the sun and known as "The Red Planet".

Jupiter

The fifth planet from the sun and the largest in our solar system.

Saturn

The sixth planet from the sun and a horder of many moons.

Uranus

The seventh planet from the sun and a cold ice world with a strange axial tilt.

Neptune

The eighth planet from the sun and the furthest planet out in our solar system.
